A. J. Hackett

NEW ZEALAND ENTREPENEUR

— THE INVENTOR OF —
BUNGEE JUMPING

A. J. Hackett

is a New Zealand entrepeneur who popularised and made safer the extreme sport of bungy jumping.

His achievements include bungy jumping form a variety of locations, including the Eiffel Tower (even recieving a short jail term for this), Auckland's Harbour Bridge among others.
