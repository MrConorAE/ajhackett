ABOUT HACKETT







A. J. Hackett was born in Pukekohe in May 1958, but grew up on Auckland's North Shore. He attended Westlake High School, where he played Rugby Union between 1972 and 1974.

He left school at the age of 16, when he joined an apprenticeship as a carpenter-joiner. During this time, he took up snowboarding and skiing. He then moved to Perth where he sold encyclopedias for four months, later returning to New Zealand to set up a construction buisness.
